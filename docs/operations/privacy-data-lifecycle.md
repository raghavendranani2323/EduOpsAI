# Privacy And Data Lifecycle

## Institution responsibilities

- Provide notices and obtain any consent required for child and guardian data.
- Add only records necessary for education administration.
- Review staff access regularly and revoke leavers promptly.
- Define retention periods for admissions, attendance, finance and
  communications records.

## Request workflow

1. Verify the requester and institution.
2. Record whether the request is access, correction, export, restriction or
   deletion.
3. Identify legal or dispute holds before deletion.
4. Export or correct only the scoped tenant records.
5. Record completion in the audit log without storing sensitive documents in
   audit metadata.

## Vendor inventory

Maintain current entries for Supabase, hosting, email/OTP, push, WhatsApp and
any error-monitoring provider. Record purpose, data categories, region,
retention and contract/contact details.

## Breach workflow

Contain access, preserve logs, rotate exposed credentials, identify affected
tenants/data, obtain qualified legal advice on notification duties, communicate
facts without speculation, and record remediation.
